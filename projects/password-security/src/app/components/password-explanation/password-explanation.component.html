Why Do We Need Strong Passwords?

A strong password is your first line of defense against hackers. It protects your personal information, accounts, and sensitive data from unauthorized access.

What Makes a Password Strong?

  • It is at least 12 characters long.
  • Includes a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Does not contain easily guessed information like your name or birthdate.

Why Weak Passwords Are Risky

Weak passwords are easy to guess using tools like brute-force attacks or by hackers who find them in leaked databases. This puts your accounts and private data at risk.

Tips for Creating Strong Passwords

  • Use a password manager to generate and store unique passwords for every account.
  • Avoid using the same password across multiple accounts.
  • Update your passwords regularly to stay secure.
  • Check if your password has been breached with tool like "Have I Been Pwned" before using it.

Remember: A strong password keeps your accounts safe!
